Purpose

Why will people come to your site? Our purpose is to give quality and cheap products to our customers in the site. The clients who are having troubles or does not have enough time to go to shopping malls our site is the place to shop with.

Audience

Our target audience are all teenagers to seniorrs since some of them doesn't want to go to malls since it's crowded or busy doing stuffs. It would help them see different kinds of products and also help them to be safe from danger in any case viruses and other sickness that they could get in crowded areas.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

We want our customers to let them know that our site is trusted and credible, since we are a shopping site we want them to check our different kinds of items we offer.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

We will give enough information of the people under the company running the site also who is the owner and the purpose and audience target of the site.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

We want them to feel the website is trusted and all the products or items offered has good quality or cheap.
